# fleet_management

Problem Statement

Managing fleet operations becomes difficult when data is scattered across different areas such as drivers, fuel usage, maintenance, and revenue. Without a centralized reporting system, it is hard for management to track operational efficiency, identify cost-heavy areas, and understand how fleet activity impacts business performance.

This project was created to solve that problem by building an interactive Power BI Fleet Management Dashboard that brings together operational and revenue-related metrics in one place.

Project Objective

The objective of this project was to design a dashboard that helps monitor fleet performance from both an operational and business perspective.

The dashboard was built to:

track driver activity and vehicle usage
measure fuel consumption and fuel efficiency
analyze maintenance and repair costs
monitor revenue and order delivery trends
understand customer ordering patterns
identify high-performing locations and business areas
Tools & Technologies Used
Power BI
DAX
Data Modeling
Power Query
Interactive Filters and Slicers
Map and KPI Visualizations
Dashboard Pages
1. Driver Dashboard

This page focuses on driver and vehicle performance. It helps monitor key operational metrics such as:

total kilometers traveled
total fuel consumed
liters consumed
maintenance cost
repair/fix cost
number of drives

It also allows analysis by truck type, trailer type, and time period.

2. Driver Tooltip

This page provides a more detailed driver-level view with calculated performance indicators such as:

fuel consumption per kilometer
fuel cost per kilometer
average fuel efficiency
average speed
total trips or drives

This helps evaluate individual driver performance more effectively.

3. Revenue Insights

This page connects fleet operations to business performance. It includes:

total revenue
delivered orders
total goods value
total weight and cubic weight
year-over-year growth
repeat and one-time customer analysis
revenue trend over time
revenue by city and state
4. Key Insights

This page summarizes the most important observations from the dashboard for quick business understanding and decision-making.

Business Questions Solved

This dashboard helps answer important business questions such as:

Which drivers are covering the highest distance?
Which drivers or vehicles are consuming the most fuel?
What is the average fuel cost per kilometer?
Which vehicles are generating high maintenance expenses?
How is revenue performing over time?
Which locations contribute the most revenue?
How many customers are repeat customers versus one-time customers?
How do truck type and trailer type affect operational performance?
Key Insights

From this dashboard, the business can identify:

drivers or vehicles with high fuel consumption
low fuel-efficiency patterns
high maintenance and repair cost areas
revenue trends across regions
delivery and order growth over time
customer retention patterns through repeat orders
operational inefficiencies impacting profitability
Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following actions can help improve business performance:

monitor drivers with unusually high fuel cost per kilometer
investigate vehicles with high repair and maintenance costs
improve driver practices to increase fuel efficiency
optimize fleet allocation based on truck and trailer performance
focus more on high-revenue cities and states
develop strategies to increase repeat customers
regularly track cost-to-revenue patterns for better profitability control
Business Impact

This dashboard helps improve visibility across fleet operations and supports data-driven decision-making. By combining operational KPIs with revenue insights, it enables management to:

reduce unnecessary operational costs
improve driver and vehicle performance
identify profitable business locations
better understand customer ordering behavior
make faster and more informed strategic decisions
Conclusion

The Fleet Management Dashboard is an end-to-end Power BI project that transforms raw fleet and delivery data into meaningful business insights. It demonstrates how data visualization and analytics can be used to improve operational efficiency, control costs, and support revenue growth in the transportation and logistics domain.
